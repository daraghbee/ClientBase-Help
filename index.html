Made for trades & field services

ClientBase — Run your day, not a spreadsheet

Fast client and job management, clean payments tracking, and weather-aware scheduling. Built for window cleaners, gardeners, and small service teams.

Core Capabilities

  • Clients: Names, phone, address, price, notes.
  • Appointments: Date, duration, completed/paid flags.
  • Recurrence: Weekly, fortnightly, six-weekly, monthly.
  • Today View: Daily timeline with quick actions.
  • Payments: Mark paid/unpaid, stats view.
  • Weather: Apple WeatherKit integration (enable in Settings).
  • Messaging: WhatsApp templates with [DATE] tokens.
  • Notes: Quick scratch pad.

Quick Start

  1. Add your first client using the + button.
  2. Create an appointment with date and duration.
  3. Enable weather in Settings → Weather and set location.
  4. Customise your WhatsApp message in Settings → Messaging.
  5. Open Today to run your route.
Tip: the bottom weather accessory shows hourly rain and temperature.

Weather Integration

To enable weather:

  1. Go to Settings → Weather.
  2. Turn on Enable Weather.
  3. Select your location.

Weather shows in Today view and the bottom bar with hourly rain. It updates automatically once per day to save battery.

FAQ

How do I confirm jobs?

Tap a job → WhatsApp icon. The message inserts the job’s [DATE]. Edit template in Settings → Messaging.

How do I handle rain?

Use the hourly forecast to reorder jobs. Long-press → Reschedule to move an appointment.

How can I repeat a job every 6 weeks?

Set recurrence to “Every 6 Weeks” when creating the appointment.

Can I change a one-off price?

Yes. Use “Price override” inside an appointment.

Does weather work offline?

Yes, the last forecast is cached. It refreshes when online again.

Why doesn’t [DATE] insert correctly?

Ensure you typed it exactly as [DATE] in your message template.

Where are my notes?

Use the Notes tab’s Scratch Pad for quick reminders.

How do I contact support?

Email support@clientbase.app with your issue and iOS version.
