Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my SMS/WhatsApp message?

In Settings, tap Message Templates. You can insert [DATE] and [CLIENT] placeholders for automatic formatting.

How do I backup or move my data?

Go to Settings, then tap Export / Import. You can export all data as a JSON file to save, or import one back into ClientBase. Data includes clients, appointments, payments, and notes.
