Frequently Asked Questions

How do I backup my data?

Go to the Clients tab, tap the menu button (three dots), and select "Export All (JSON)". Save the file to your preferred location.

Can I customize the SMS confirmation message?

Yes! In the Today view, tap the gear icon and customize your message template. Use [DATE] to automatically insert the appointment date/time.

How do I mark an appointment as complete?

In the Today view, tap on an appointment to expand it, then tap the "Complete" button. The appointment will be marked with a green checkmark.

What happens to completed appointments?

Completed appointments remain in your history. They appear with a strikethrough and show the completion time. This helps track client visits and earnings.

Can I group clients by tags?

Yes! In the Clients tab, tap the menu button and select "Tag" under "Group By". Your clients will be organized by their assigned tags.

Is my data stored securely?

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure Core Data framework. No data is sent to external servers.

Troubleshooting

Import Issues

If you're having trouble importing data:

  • Ensure the JSON file is properly formatted
  • Check that client names are not empty
  • Verify the file was exported from ClientBase

WhatsApp Integration

For WhatsApp to work:

  • WhatsApp must be installed on your device
  • Phone numbers should include country codes
  • Remove any special characters from phone numbers
